FSC Mixtapes

Welcome to our Mixtapes. These are some curated recordings we like, and we’ve done quite a lot of editing and volume adjustment to give you a lovely listening experience.

You can stream them, but we’d really suggest listening to them on an audiobook player such as BookPlayer - you can download them to your phone as chapterised MP3s and play via bluetooth / CarPlay and because they’re chapterised you can easily skip or repeat tracks.
